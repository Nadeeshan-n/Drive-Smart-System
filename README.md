#  Drive Smart (Car Rental Management System)

##  Project Overview
The **Car Rental Management System** is a Java-based application developed to assist car rental companies in managing their daily operations efficiently.

This system is designed mainly for **company-side management**, where staff members handle vehicle rentals, customer information, and rental records through a local interface. It is **not an online booking system**; customers select vehicles at the rental office using the system interface.

---

##  Objectives
- Manage vehicles and their availability
- Maintain customer information
- Handle rental and return processes
- Track rental history
- Improve company operational efficiency
- Apply Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) concepts in a real-world solution

---

##  Features
- Vehicle registration and management
- Customer registration and management
- Rental booking and return handling
- Vehicle availability tracking
- Rental record management
- Exception handling for safer operations


---

##  System Scope
The system focuses on **internal company management** rather than online reservations. All operations are handled by company staff within the office.
